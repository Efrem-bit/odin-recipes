Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground meet
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the sauce with onion, garlic, meat (or vegetables), and tomato sauce.
  2. Prepare the béchamel or ricotta mixture.
  3. Layer sauce, pasta sheets, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  5. Bake until golden and bubbly.
