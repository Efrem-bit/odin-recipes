Pizza

Description

Mix flour, water, yeast, and salt to make a soft dough. Let it rise until doubled, then stretch it into a round. Spread tomato sauce, add mozzarella, and your favorite toppings. Bake in a very hot oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Ingredients

  1. Mix flour, water, yeast, and salt.
  2. Knead into a smooth dough.
  3. Let it rise until doubled in size.
  4. Stretch the dough into a round.
  5. Add tomato sauce, mozzarella, and toppings.
  6. Bake in a very hot oven until golden and bubbly.
