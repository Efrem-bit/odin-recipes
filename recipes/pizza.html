Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a baked flatbread base, topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of savory ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, yeast, and salt.
  2. Knead into a smooth dough.
  3. Let it rise until doubled in size.
  4. Stretch the dough into a round.
  5. Add tomato sauce, mozzarella, and toppings.
  6. Bake in a very hot oven until golden and bubbly.
