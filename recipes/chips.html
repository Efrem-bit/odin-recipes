Chips

Description

Fried chips are crispy slices or sticks of potato, deep-fried until golden and crunchy, and lightly salted.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into slices or sticks.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or fryer.
  3. Fry the potatoes until golden and crispy.
  4. Drain excess oil and sprinkle with salt.
